Instructions

Step 1

Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.

Step 2

Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.

Step 3

Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.

Step 4

Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.

Step 5

Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.

ingredients

Ground Beef

Spaghetti Sauce

Cottage Cheese, Mozarella, and Paremsan

Eggs

Dried Parsley, Salt, and Black Pepper

Lasagna Noodles

½ cup of water

Lasagna

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. The same-named Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.
