Instructions

Step 1

Arrange the steak fries in a single layer in 2 greased 15x10x1-in. baking pans. Bake at 450Â° for 15-18 minutes or until tender and golden brown.

Step 2

Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, combine the soup, milk, garlic powder and onion powder; heat through. Drizzle over fries; sprinkle with paprika.

ingredients

1 package (28 ounces) frozen steak fries

1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cheddar cheese soup, undiluted

1/4 cup 2% milk

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Paprika

Cheese Fries

The dish known as "cheese fries" or "cheesy chips" is made up of French fries dipped in cheese and maybe topped with additional ingredients. Typically, cheese fries are offered for lunch or dinner. They are mostly seen in English-speaking nations in fast-food restaurants, eateries, and barbecues.
